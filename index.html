Welcome to my web page

I am a researcher at UCSF Global Health Group, working on the Malaria Elimination Initiative. I specialize in developing and implementing probabilistic models that aim to contribute a better understanding of the drivers of infectious diseases as well as health care access of the population.

I am interested in applications of probabilistic modeling to risk assessment and prevention, particularly in the topics of health and natural disasters.
