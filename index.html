Welcome to my web page

I am a researcher at UCSF Global Health Group, working on the Malaria Elimination Initiative. I speciallize in developing and implementing spatial models that aim to contribute a better understanding of the drivers of malaria infections as well as health care access of the population.

In general, I am interested in applications of probabilistic modeling to risk assessment and prevention, in topics like epidemiology or natural disasters.
