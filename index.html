Welcome to my web page

At the moment and for a brief period, I am collaborating as a Research Partner at the UN Global Pulse - Kampla. I work with a very interesting group of data scientists, trying to devolop new tools to analyze and visualize infectious diseases data. The idea is to contribute with the Health Service in Uganda, providing them accurate and timely information. This is a continuation of my PhD studies in the ML@SITraN research group, lead by Prof. Neil Lawrence.

In general, I am interested in applications of probabilistic modelling to risk assessment and prevention -like epidemiology or natural disasters-.
